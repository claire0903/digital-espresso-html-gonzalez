Our History: Definition List

Arabica
- A type of good-quality coffee or coffee beans.
Robusta
- Coffee or coffee beans from a widely grown kind of coffee plant. Beans of this variety are often used in the manufacture of instant coffee.
Fair Trade
- Certified sustainable sourcing model that ensures coffee farmers in developing countries receive fair, stable, and above-market prices for their crops.
